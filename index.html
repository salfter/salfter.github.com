Scott Alfter's Gentoo Overlay

This is a collection of ebuilds I've written or modified over the years to get the apps I want into Gentoo. Some may have been superseded by other overlays or the main Portage tree, as many of these are version bumps I've based off of existing ebuilds to get the latest-and-greatest of something. A few ebuilds (such as www-apps/concerto and net-p2p/litecoin) are AFAIK not available elsewhere.

Adding to Layman

As of 29 March 2015, this overlay is now available through layman. Assuming that you already have layman installed and configured, adding this overlay takes just one step:

  1. Pull in my overlay:
    layman -a salfter
